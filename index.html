About:

This is a 1v1 platformer, arena style, king of the hill, game where players compete to capture a platform for a specific amount of time. Players can pull off combos, manuever around threats, and become king of the platforms. The player who has scored 1000 points wins the game.

How To Play: Stand on a platform and watch your score go up! Get 1000 Points to WIN.

Player One Controls:
Movement: W, A, S, D
Combo Keys: R,T,Y
Combos:
Super Jump: R + R
Shoot: T + T
Bomb: Y + Y
Player Two Controls:
Movement: Up, Down, Left, Right
Combo Keys: I,O,P
Combos:
Super Jump: I + I
Shoot: O + O
Bomb: P + P
